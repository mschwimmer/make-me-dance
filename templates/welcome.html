How do you choose which songs to use?

Spotify has their own secret formula, much like the Krabby Patty from Spongebob.
However, this secret formula doesn't create the best sandwich in Bikini Bottom.
No, this formula creates a "Danceability Score" for each song.
1 is the most danceable song in the world (think David Bowie - Best Of).
0 is the least danceable song in the world (think David Bowie - Worst Of).


Let's look at some examples.

Al Green - Let's Stay Together

Spotify gives this song a 0.756

Claude Debussy - Clair de Lune

Spotify gives this song a 0.320

At the end of the day, everyone grooves differently

I've been known to bob my head to Debussy myself.
